# Anchor Marianas — company one-pager

_Scannable in under two minutes. Product-first. Gets the meeting before the deck._

**Anchor Marianas LLC** · Guam, building toward Malaysia · adam@anchormarianas.com · anchormarianas.com/scan

---

**What we are.** Anchor builds **AnchorScan**, a proprietary AI diagnostic for small businesses. Point it at any business and it reads that business's public signals, its website and its Google reviews, and returns a plain-language diagnosis of where it is losing customers, plus the fixes. Live and free at anchormarianas.com/scan.

**The problem we own.** SMBs across the Pacific and ASEAN are surrounded by signals they cannot read. The AI that could read them is built for engineers, not for the owner of a hotel or a repair shop. AnchorScan is the translation layer for people who do not code.

**Why it is a product, not an agency.** The defensible asset is the diagnostic method, how raw public signal becomes an owner-legible read, tuned per vertical. Services (a free site, a paid AI receptionist) are how the product reaches the market, not the company.
