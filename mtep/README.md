# Anchor Marianas — MTEP application pack

Assets for the Malaysia Tech Entrepreneur Programme (MTEP, New Tech Entrepreneur track) application. Built to present Anchor as a company building **proprietary technology** (AnchorScan), not an AI agency, which is the single framing that decides this application.

## The one rule everything obeys
MTEP's New track disqualifies consulting, reselling, integration, and customizing platforms for clients. So across every asset:
- **AnchorScan is the company.** It is the proprietary product at the center.
- The AI Reception Pilot, audits, and services are **distribution and revenue for the product**, never the identity.
- Nothing says "AI agency" or "paying AI client." Hilton is a **delivered web product and reference**. Anchor is **pre-revenue on the AI-services line**, stated plainly.

## What is in here
- `pitch-deck.md` — the 10-section MDEC pitch deck content. The make-or-break artifact. Turn into slides.
- `one-pager.md` — a 2-minute company brief that gets the meeting before the deck.
- `gtm-narrative.md` — the go-to-market narrative, funnel math, and a pipeline snapshot template.
- Evaluator page: `public/company.html` -> served at `/company`. Point MDEC to this URL, not the customer homepage.

## Two hard gates (quality is irrelevant without them). Only you can do these.
1. **RM50,000** held in a personal account across **3 months** of statements. Move it now so the 3-month clock is already running.
2. A signed **12-month Malaysia Digital Hub (or MRANTI) agreement** before you submit.
3. Verify current fees, paid-up capital, and any 2026 rule changes on the live portal: mtep.mdec.com.my.

## What only you can supply (placeholders marked `[...]` in the docs)
- Proof of funds figure and bank statements.
- Company incorporation status and any Malaysian entity plan.
- Real pipeline names and stages (keep them true: contacted / scan delivered / pilot proposed / LOI / signed).
- The specific Malaysia Digital Hub you sign with.
- Any real AnchorScan usage or scan counts to date (show if real, never invent).
