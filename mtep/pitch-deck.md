## 1. Problem
Small businesses across the Pacific and ASEAN are drowning in signals they cannot read. Every week their customers tell them exactly what is wrong, in reviews, in unanswered calls, in a website that turns people away, and the owner has no way to hear it or act on it. The AI that could read those signals is built for engineers and enterprises, not for the person running a hotel or a repair shop.

## 2. Solution
**AnchorScan**: a proprietary AI diagnostic that reads a business's public signals (its website and its Google reviews) and returns a plain-language read of where it is losing customers, plus the specific fixes. Free to run, in under a minute, in the owner's language. It is a product, not a consulting service.

## 3. Product and technology (spend the most here, keep it jargon-free)
- **What it does, plainly:** point AnchorScan at any business. It pulls that business's website text and its Google Maps reviews, runs them through a language model with a purpose-built diagnostic method, and returns evidence-backed observations (each tied to a real review), the questions the owner should answer, and where to focus. No invented numbers, no dashboard, no pitch.
- **What is proprietary:** the diagnostic method and the report structure, the way raw public signal becomes an owner-legible read. That method, tuned per vertical, is the defensible asset, not the underlying model.
